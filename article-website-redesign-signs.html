7 Signs Your Website Is Losing Customers

A website can look “okay” and still lose business every day. The signs are usually visible in speed, mobile layout, messaging and enquiry flow.

1. Your Website Loads Slowly

If visitors wait too long, they leave before reading your offer. Compress images, remove heavy scripts and keep the layout clean.

2. Mobile Experience Feels Broken

Most Indian customers check businesses on phones. Text must be readable, buttons easy to tap and forms short.

3. There Is No Proof

No reviews, no work samples, no owner intro and no guarantee means visitors have no reason to trust you over competitors.

4. Your Content Does Not Answer Questions

Customers want pricing, timeline, service area, process and results. If your website hides these answers, they will ask someone else.
